About Matrix

Team Matrix was born from late-night discussions and a shared passion for cybersecurity. Founded by a group of security enthusiasts at SCIT, Pune.

Guided by the values and principles of esteemed communities like ISACA, we aim to inspire confidence and enable innovation through technology. Our collaboration with Null, the Open Security Community, reflects our commitment to raising security awareness, advancing cutting-edge research, and creating a centralized knowledge platform for cybersecurity professionals and enthusiasts.

Team Matrix has been recognized for its dedication, with notable accolades like being named the Cybersecurity Club of the Month by MCCIA in January 2025. Together, we continue to lead and inspire, working toward a secure and resilient digital future.

About ISACA

Founded in 1969, ISACA is a global professional association focused on IT governance, risk management, and cybersecurity. With more than 140,000 members worldwide, ISACA empowers professionals by providing them with resources, certifications, and training to navigate the complexities of modern IT governance, cybersecurity, and risk management.

ISACA offers industry-leading certifications like CISA, CISM, and CRISC, which have become globally recognized benchmarks for expertise in IT auditing, information security, and risk management. Through its dedicated chapters and events, ISACA fosters a collaborative community that inspires innovation, networking, and knowledge sharing.

About Null

Null is India's largest open security community, formed with the vision to create awareness about information security and provide a collaborative platform for individuals to learn, share, and grow together. With chapters spread across various cities, Null has become a pivotal community for security enthusiasts and professionals.

Founded in 2007, Null's mission is to promote knowledge sharing, innovation, and skills development through hands-on workshops, meetups, and CTF (Capture The Flag) events. The community engages in critical discussions around security research, ethical hacking, and the latest cyber threats, making it one of the most influential security communities in India.
